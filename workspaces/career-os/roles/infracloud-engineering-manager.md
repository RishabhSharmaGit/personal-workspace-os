## Company snapshot

InfraCloud Technologies — **cloud-native consulting / services** company: Kubernetes, DevOps, platform engineering, SRE, and application development for clients. Pune-based, remote-friendly. Indeed rating 2.5/5 (small sample). This is a **services-delivery EM** role — client-facing, runs delivery across **Fixed Scope and T&M** engagement models, manages 10+ engineers.

## The role

**Engineering Manager** owning **end-to-end project delivery** (planning → execution), acting as the **primary point of contact for clients and delivery teams**. People + delivery leadership: mentoring, status reporting, milestone/risk/schedule tracking, effort estimation, stakeholder feedback loops, process establishment, plus org-wide initiatives to improve delivery-management practice. Drives **architecture & scalability** discussions. 10+ yrs, manage 10+, infra-eng or app-dev background.

## Tailoring strategy (delivery + cloud-native forward)

1. **Headline = "Engineering Manager / Tech Lead — Cloud-Native Platforms & Delivery"** — lead delivery + cloud-native, not healthcare.
2. **Summary** opens with leadership (2+ yrs leading 5-8 + QA) + **end-to-end delivery ownership** + **cloud-native/platform-engineering depth** (Cloud Run, K8s/GKE, Terraform, CI/CD, OpenTelemetry).
3. **Nova = leadership/delivery anchor** — led/mentored team, roadmap with PM/CTO, best practices, hiring, 90% issue cut, integration framework.
4. **Confido** — tilt bullets toward **architecture + cloud-native re-architecture + reliability/observability + delivery throughput + hiring bar**; keep gen-AI/voice to one "hands-on with cutting-edge" line (not the lead).
5. **Skills**: "Leadership & delivery" first, then **Cloud-native & DevOps** very prominent (the match), then languages/backend/data/observability.
6. **Surface delivery-management vocabulary** truthfully: planning→execution, milestones, effort estimation, status/roadmap, stakeholder alignment, architecture & scalability reviews.

## Honesty notes (for interview prep)

- **Team of 10+**: own that you've directly led 5-8 + a QA; speak to coordinating across a larger org and readiness to scale span-of-control. Don't claim 10+ on the resume.
- **Consulting / T&M / Fixed-Scope**: be upfront you're product-built; map your delivery-ownership, estimation, and stakeholder skills onto the engagement-management model; express genuine interest in the services motion.
- **Client-facing**: frame your customer-facing founding-eng exposure (90+ clinics) + PM/CTO stakeholder work; be honest that account-level client ownership would be a growth area.
