## Tailoring strategy (leadership-forward)

1. **Lead with leadership identity** — headline "Engineering Leader / Tech Lead", summary opens with "2+ years leading a team of 5-8 engineers + QA" + delivery + best-practices culture.
2. **Nova is the anchor role** — expand its leadership bullets (led/mentored team, roadmap with PM/CTO, established best practices, 90% issue reduction, integration framework). Put delivery + people signals first.
3. **Confido** — frame founding-engineer leadership: architecture ownership, set the hiring bar (interview framework), cross-stack mentoring, shipping under startup pressure.
4. **Surface Java** (Seclore J2EE) + **enterprise SaaS** (Seclore) + **fintech-adjacent** (Nova insurance/payments) to hit the preferred bullets.
5. **De-emphasize** deep voice-AI specifics — keep one line (modern AI/LLM is a "cutting-edge" plus the JD values) but don't lead with it; this is a leadership + backend + fintech role.
6. **Add a "Leadership & delivery" skills line** first (team size, mentoring, hiring, sprint/Agile, roadmap, code-review/testing culture), then technical skills (Java/TypeScript/cloud-native forward).

## Honesty notes (for interview prep)

- **People management**: be ready to speak to leading 5-8 engineers + QA as Tech Lead — what you owned (delivery, mentoring, hiring bar, unblocking) vs. what's new at EM level (formal performance reviews, career-ladder ownership, headcount planning). Don't claim formal perf-review ownership on the resume; frame as "led + mentored + grew engineers".
- **Domain**: own that you're fintech-adjacent (insurance/payments) + enterprise SaaS, and express genuine interest in capital markets — fast learner, regulated-domain experience (HIPAA, data security) transfers.
